Series

Body & Femininity

A series about self-acceptance, softness and the quiet power of real bodies. Here, femininity is not performed for perfection, but allowed to exist as it truly is – vulnerable, strong, unfinished and deeply human. Through these images, the body becomes both subject and sculpture, carrying invisible stories of doubt, resilience and gentle strength.
